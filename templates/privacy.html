Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At BANKXY, your privacy and data security are our highest priorities. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle the information you provide while using our secure bank statement conversion services.

2. Zero-Retention Policy for Financial Data

We do not store, retain, or share your bank statements, PDF files, or the converted Excel files. All files are processed completely in memory and are instantly deleted from our servers the moment the conversion process finishes.

3. Data We Collect

We collect minimal usage data strictly for system reliability and analytics, which may include:

  • The bank format selected or auto-detected.
  • Conversion success or failure status (to improve our parser accuracy).
  • Email addresses (only if you choose to sign up or contact us).

This analytics data contains no personal financial information.

4. Security Measures

All data transmitted to and from BANKXY is encrypted using industry-standard protocols. Our processing environment is secured to ensure that your financial data is protected from unauthorized access during the brief moment of processing.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact.bankxy@gmail.com.
